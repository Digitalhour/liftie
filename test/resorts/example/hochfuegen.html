Lifts in Operation

In the Hochfügen ski area, twelve modern lifts carry you safely and comfortably up to elevations as high as 2500 metres above sea level.
From mid November until the end of April, the Hochfügen ski area, with its twelve different lifts (chair, gondola and tow lifts), promises you a perfect skiing experience in an incomparable alpine setting. Here’s a quick, easy overview of which lifts are currently running.

Visit the Slopes for Your Friday!
Stop by Snow Valley today to get a taste of winter and take your first runs of 2014 with us! In addition to skiing/boarding, we've got lessons and snowplay in the daytime, and even a skiing/boarding session under the lights planned for this evening. We'll see you soon!

Holiday Night sessions will be available nightly through December 30 and January 2 through January 4. Night session hours are 3-9 p.m. Enjoy an evening of skiing and/or snowboarding under the lights!

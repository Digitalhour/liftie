Advance Purchase

Like saving money? No matter when you plan to ski or ride, purchase in advance and save! Don't forget when you purchase lift tickets, equipment rentals and ski/snowboard lessons at least 7 days in advance & save an extra 10%!!

Wapiti Wildlife Center

Warm up and learn!  Drop in to the Wapiti Wildlife Center at the top of the Elk Camp Lift on Snowmass. Warm up, have some hot chocolate and learn a bit about local ecology. Ski a run with a naturalist from the Aspen Center for Environmental Studies. Free guided tours every day at 11 am and 1 pm.

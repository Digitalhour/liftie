Hello skiers and riders,

Winter storm Nemo is heading our way with snow expected to start falling Friday morning and continue into Saturday morning.  It's going to be a powder weekend at Hunter Mountain!  Check back for updates on snow accumulation and conditions once the storm begins.

Take your love to new heights this Valentine’s Day at Hunter Mountain!  We are arranging for couples to renew their vows at the summit with a special ceremony. The offer is extended to any married couple with lift tickets or season passes who are able to ski or ride from the top of the mountain.  Everyone participating will meet on the summit deck at 10:30am.  Reverend Karen Monk, of the Kaaterskill United Methodist Church will be there to officiate the ceremony. After the “I Do's”, the group will be treated to a small reception at the summit lodge. For those who want to extend their experience into an overnight stay, the Kaatskill Mountain Club has special rates for the night of February 13th or 14th.  Guests who participate in the vow renewal ceremony and spend the night in The Kaatskill Mountain Club will receive a complimentary champagne split and a special discount on a couple’s massage at the Mountain Club Spa.You must register in advance for this offer by visiting this site.

Our lifts are open from 9:00am - 4pm. The Empire Terrain Park is open with a great build. Check out the setup at RideHunter.com. The Progression Park, located on Hunter One, is also open for our terrain park beginners. Don't forget, New York Zipline Adventure Tours operate year round.  Please call ahead to make your reservation.

Enjoy your day!

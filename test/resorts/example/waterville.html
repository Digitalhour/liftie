Saturday 1/6/18 - Update; Upper Mountain Lifts and Valley Run are on hold until further notice due to very high winds both at the base, and the summit. 

We will open the lower mountain at 8AM, with a powder/packed powder surface. The FunRun and People's Park are ready for your jibbing enjoyment. We're expecting high winds to continue for most of the day; please plan accordingly and wear your facemasks and extra layers if you're joining us. With low ambient temps, and high wind chill - frostbite can occur quickly. Make sure to stay covered up out there!

The glades still have plenty of untracked stashes - explore some new zones and find a new line in Narnia, The Sugar Shack Glades, and beyond. We also have over 50K of open Nordic terrain - try out some skinny skis in Town Square and be sure to explore our interactive trail map for the most up-to-date terrain and weather information. 

T-Bars, Buckets, Sunnyside, The Waffle Cabin, and the Marketplace are open for all of your food and beverage needs, plus live Apres entertainment and great food and beverage specials every weekend! 

If you missed out on the fun, be sure to buy your tickets for the next Starry Nights Dinner at Sunnyside Timberlodge - happening on January 13th. Call 236-8311 x3000 

 

Stay warm out there, I'll see you on the hill! 

Mike Cuddihy - Snow Reporter

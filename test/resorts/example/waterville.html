Today's Notes

7:33pm, Sunday 13th January 2013
Updates filed after 3:00 PM reflect the next day's conditions.
Join us for Funday Monday and save $20 on all lift tickets! Grab first chair at 9am for skiing and riding on 49 trails. Mother Nature took a swing at us with some warms temps today but we're still going strong with a 20-28 inch base and more snow to come as temps drop after the next few days. South Street Park is now open! South Street is home to mid-sized features for all you rippers out there working your way up to Exhibition, or just looking for a fun jam sesh. South Street was the place to be this Saturday as riders and skiers tested their skills on the features. Cross Country skiers enjoy 58km of trails now open in the Valley! Check out the Waterville Valley Resort Nordic Center for more details! Stay up-to-date with all things Waterville Valley Resort on our Twitter and Facebook pages. Think Snow!

What's Happening

Curious George and his gang including the man in the yellow hat, bananas, and even Bruce the Moose will be popping up all over Waterville Valley Resort next Saturday, January 19th at the Curious George Cottage Winter Family Festival. Get your tickets today through the Rey Center at www.thereycenter.org. Join us for a night of laughter at the 2nd Annual Lenny Clarke Comedy Fest to benefit the Wounded Warriors and the Waterville Valley Adaptive Sports Program. Lenny will be joined by America's Got Talent runner-up Tom Cotter and his wife, comedian Kerri Louise Cotter, Paul DeAngelo, Graig Murphy, Tony V., Dave Russo, and others. Tickets are just $30 and can be purchased online. Check the Waterville Valley Resort upcoming events page for more details on all events! Lift tickets are now available for purchase online - buy now and save $10!

Snow Report

December 9 

CONDITIONS

Lifts will be spinning today from 9am - 4pm.  You can expect to be skiing and riding on 21 trails and four lifts with machine groomed/packed powder conditions.

Look for some fun, new terrain that has been added recently, like Kandahar, Ledges and Lower Skimeister.

Snowmaking was back at it this week, plus the snowfall from early last week was killer, so conditions are expected to be pretty great out there.

Today's weather looks like mostly sunny, with a high near 30°, with wind chill values as low as 3° around 9:00am and southwest wind around 5 mph. With lower wind chill values, make sure you dress in layers and take breaks as needed.

Lift ticket rates are $65 for adults, $55 for teens and $45 for juniors and seniors at the ticket window. Purchase your tickets online, the night before your visit for a discounted rate. Discount only available online. All lift ticket rates are subject to change.

MOUNTAIN ADVENTURE PARK

The Tubing and Mountain Adventure Park will be open this Saturday and Sunday, 10am - 4pm with five lanes of tubing.  Two hours of tubing or unlimited rides in the Mountain Adventure Park is $29, or you can try both for $49. 

FOOD & BEVERAGE 

 Legend's Grille in the main base lodge, Zip's Pub in the base area, the Meister Hut at the summit and Slider's Cafe at the tubing hill will be open this weekend.

SNOWMAKING

Snowmakers fired up last night on the Comp Slope, Koessler, Lower Koessler, Middle, North Slope, the Reel, Gentoo and the Beginner Basin. Stay up to date with our snowmaking progress on our Snowmageddon Blog!

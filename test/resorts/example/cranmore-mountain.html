Monday, December 9th PROJECTION FOR THE WEEKEND 12-14-13

We hope you enjoyed another weekend here at Cranmore!

We will be closed for the remainder of the week blowing snow every chance we get (If you're interested in reading more about our snowmaking progress, check out our Snowmageddon Blog at Cranmore.com). We open for daily operations Saturday, December 14th. For Saturday we hope to be skiing and riding 23 trails, with a little help from Mother Nature and are awesome Team Snowmageddon.

Our Mountain Adventure Park and Tubing Park will also re-open Saturday, December 14th, with a couple more tubing trails.

Again, this is a projected snow report, please check back throughout the week and Saturday morning for the up to date report.  Thanks for another great weekend, we'll see you soon!

December 9, 2013 Update

Happy Monday.

Mother Nature is providing a few inches of snow today which is making everything look nice and white.

Our snowmakers on the other hand are dropping more than a few inches on our trails thanks to round the clock snowmaking since Saturday.  I toured the mountain this morning with Cranmore's Snowsurfaces Manager Johnny Mersereau and it looks like we will be adding 6 trails and 23.7 acres to our snow report for the weekend.  That brings us to 23 total trails and close to 80 acres of ski terrain ready for the upcoming weekend.  We are closed this Monday through Friday to maximize snowmaking.  This Saturday we will be open daily for the rest of the season.

Look for Upper Hurricane, Middle, the Competition Slope, the Lower North, Upper Beginner's Luck and Outta Luck to open.

With the cold weather forecasted this week we will start firing up guns on the Ledges and down Skimeister on Friday of this week.  We will be well into Phase IV at that point so things continue to progress very well.

This year's Christmas Week will be looking very white at Cranmore!

Ben Wilcox
President & GM

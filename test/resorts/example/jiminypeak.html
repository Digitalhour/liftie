Snow Report

» Last Update: 1:24pm, Friday 20th December 2013

Friday Day #17. Operating hours 9 am - 10 pm. Lift tickets are at non-holiday rates. All open terrain groomed. Coming up - Sun, Dec 22 is Potter Brothers Demo Day.

Happy Friday! We are skiing and riding our way into the weekend on 33 trails and 4 lifts during the day today and 21 trails and 3 lifts tonight. Grommetville terrain park is open. Operating hours for the day are 9am – 10 pm and lift tickets are at non-holiday rates.

Today’s weather calls for cloudy skies with a chance of rain moving in this afternoon and highs in the low 40s. 

Looking ahead to the weekend -  Sunday is Potter Brothers Demo Day! Come try out the latest gear from top manufacturers including Rossignol, Atomic, Line, Volkl, Blizzard, Fischer and Ramp. Demos will be available from 9 am – 2 pm and are free of charge – a valid lift ticket, and ID or credit card are required for deposit.

1st Tracks for Season Pass Holders has been postponed originally scheduled for Sunday (December 22nd) has been postponed until Sunday, January 5th.

Snowmaking Blog

Dec 20, 2013

We'll Be Back on Monday!

We’ve shut down snowmaking with an anticipated re-start day of Monday. The plan, starting on Monday, is to dust and run with the strategy of hitting every trail that we make snow on a couple times, prior to Christmas. That will allow us to restore the surface to the awesome machine groomed packed powder conditions we have had. Don’t let the weather deter your or affect your decision to come skiing -  we are going to have awesome snow!

Merry Christmas & Happy Holidays from all of us on Team Snowmageddon!

-Jim

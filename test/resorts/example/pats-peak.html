Snow Report

Our team is busy getting the mountain ready for the upcoming winter season! Work continues on our latest expansion: Operation Cascade Basin! The new triple chairlift is coming along nicely. For this season, there will be 4 new ski trails as well as 1 new glade - with 100% snowmaking coverage. Other upgrades include the installation of 6 additional snowmaking fan guns and an electrical capacity upgrade to improve output. The rental shop has added new skis, snowboards and ski boots to the inventory.

Don't forget - during "The Other Season of Pats Peak", we are a perfect location for a wedding, anniversary party, birthday party, company outing, business meeting or any other social gathering. Check out our Banquet Center section or call 1-888-PATS PEAK, ext. 123 for more information or to arrange a visit of our facility.

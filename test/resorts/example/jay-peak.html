Snow Report

Sunday December 9 at 8:03 AM

Much warmer temps and another 1-3” of new snow from the night will set us up well for today’s skiing & riding. It’s actually still lightly snowing, although another partly sunny day was forecasted, and the mercury is quickly approaching the upper teens.

We’ll have almost the same lift schedule as yesterday, and a similar trail count, with the addition of Lower Can-Am. For upper mountain lifts, the Jet Triple, Bonaventure Quad and Aerial Tram are scheduled to spin starting at 8:30 AM. For lower mountain, we’ll have the Metro Quad, Taxi Quad, and both the Stateside & Tramside Moving Carpets scheduled for 8:00 AM. All lifts will shut down today at 4:00 PM.

Our Nordic Center reopened yesterday with 100% of their trails, and about 9Km of groomed track. Find more info about our Nordic center at: https://jaypeakresort.com/skiing-riding/nordic-center

Lift tickets are $89 for adults (ages 19+) and $69 for juniors (ages 6-18) and in case you need to purchase a lift ticket, you can do so at either Tramside or Stateside Customer Service, or you can purchase online and save a little coin. CLICK HERE to purchase your lift ticket online.

A friendly reminder that in case you still need to pick-up your season pass, please head on over to Tramside Customer Service.

Current Weather

Thursday January 3rd, 2013 | Updated 9:12 am
10°F-12°C

Partly Sunny

Excellent skiing and riding conditions the past few days as our 7 day snow total has been hovering around the 50” mark, but that’s soon to increase as our Mountain Forecaster is calling for measurable snow starting tonight into Friday. Temps today have improved slightly since yesterday with single digits at the summit and teens at the base, but it’s still not a bad idea to grab a pack of hand warmers and a facemask.

Today you will be skiing or riding on 72 trails and glades with all 9 lifts scheduled to spin.  Lower Mountain lifts are scheduled to spin at 8:30a and Upper Mountain at 9:00a. We dropped the prices of our lift tickets this season: $69 for adults (ages 19+) and $55 for juniors (ages 6-18). They’re even less when you reload them online. Learn how at jaypeakresort.com.

NORDIC: The Nordic Center is open (8:30a – 4p) with a variety of beginner, intermediate and expert terrain.

Launch down La Chute or surf the flowrider today at the Pump House Indoor Waterpark. The Pump House is open today from 10:00am to 9:00pm.  Day passes are $35 for adults (ages 15+) and $25 for juniors (ages 4-14). Kids 3 and under splash for free. PLEASE NOTE: Pump House access is only guaranteed if you have pre-purchased online. Click HERE for tickets.

Stop up at  the new Jay Peak Provisions & General Store where you can find a  variety of quality grocery items, fresh meats and sandwiches at our full-service deli, a diverse wine room and fully stocked walk-in beer cave. The Jay Peak Provisions & General Store is open today from 7:00am to 10:00pm.

UPCOMING EVENTS:

January 8th: Thule Tuesday. Ski or ride Jay Peak for $40 just for having a piece of Thule hardware on your vehicle. Click HERE for more details.

January 19th:  An Evening with Bruce Hornsby.  Live in the Foeger Ballroom.  Purchase tickets HERE.

WEEKLY EVENTS:

Thursday- Brewery Tours at Kingdom Brewing Co. Buses depart from the Hotel Jay at 5:00p. Tickets are $15 and include round transportation, sampling of delicious microbrews and a Kingdom Brewing Co. commemorative Pint glass. Dial extension 2160 to book.

Saturday- Come hear  jazz music from the Chris Peterman Trio live in the Tower Bar from 3:00 to 6:00p.

Have a great day.

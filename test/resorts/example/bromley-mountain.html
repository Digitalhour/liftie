November 13, 2013

Snowmaking has been productive... VERY productive!  So much so that... that.... wait for it... WE'RE OPENING THIS WEEKEND!  Yes, you heard me correctly.  We'll open this Saturday from 9am-4pm with skiing top-to-bottom on Upper and Lower Twister.  This is the earliest opening in a long time and we couldn't be more excited.  Welcome to winter!

Details, details, details.  The Sun Mountain Express will spin from 9am-4pm both Saturday and Sunday.  Skiing/riding will be for intermediate/advanced skiers and riders only (there is no easy way down).  Lift ticket rates will be $49 for the day, and there will be no 1/2 day option (although we will offer the Sunday PM Passport for $32).  The Kids' Center will be open for day care, but we will not be giving any Ski School Lessons.  All Season Passes will be valid this weekend.

The operating plan at this point (of course this is weather permitting) is to operate weekends only through November 24th, and then open for full-time operations on Friday, November 29th.  

The weather for the weekend is looking perfect.  Temps are expected to climb back into the 40's both days with sunshine for Saturday and Partly cloudy skies for Sunday.  Sounds like the perfect weekend to dust off that gear, find that snowsuit, whatever it takes to enjoy our opening winter weekend.  

Can you tell that we're excited!?  Hope to see you soon.

-Michael

Of Vermont ski resorts, Bromley offers great ski deals and ski packages for family ski vacations. Close to Manchester Vermont, Bromley offers top skiing and snowboarding, plus plenty of shopping and other activities for a memorable family ski vacation at a great value. Ski Magazine agrees, voting Bromley #1 for Family Programs and #3 for Value in the East!

While Bromley gets stars for family ski resorts for winter ski holidays, summer is packed with summer vacation activities. For Vermont family vacations, Bromley's Sun Mountain Adventure Park is Vermont's premier amusement park. Featuring the half mile long Sun Mountain Flyer ziprider - the longest zip line in New England. More fun with our Alpine Slide, 18 HoleMiniature Golf, and Big Splash Water Slide Ride, along with the Kids Fun Parkand many more attractions. Bromley is the Vermont amusement park to be at this summer.

Whether you’re looking for great ski deals and packages at the best Vermont ski resorts in winter, or summer holiday deals for summer vacation activities, Bromley Mountain is the Vermont family resort of choice.

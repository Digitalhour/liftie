Galtür
Galtür is one of the most snow-assured areas in the Alps. An extra special winter holiday awaits guests here. While in summer Galtür invites you to go hiking & mountain biking.
Galtür
Kappl
Kappl is a winter paradise and invites you to go skiing, and tobogganing. In summer guests can head out on family-friendly and challenging hikes on the sunny hillsides.

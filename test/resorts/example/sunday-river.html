Snowmakers, On Your Marks.

Nights are getting colder. Snowmakers are getting ancy. It's only a matter of time before Mother Nature turns down the thermostat and we're blasting the snowguns. Earlier in October we tested the snowmaking system, including the new river pipeline, which will boost snowmaking by 15% this winter and set a foundation for a project that will eventually double Sunday River's snowmaking capacity. With all systems go, we're ready to dive into winter at the first sign of sustained cold.

So when is opening day? Our plans are the same as they are every year -- we'll open as early as we possibly can. That's our commitment to our season passholders. All we need is a sustained window of cold weather and we'll be ready to go for it.

What To Expect on Opening Day

As soon as we get the go-ahead from Mother Nature, the opening plan kicks in. We'll start with snowmaking on T2 and Upper Sunday Punch at . If temperatures allow, we'll work towards lower elevation snowmaking on Ecstasy, Sunday Punch, and Right Stuff. We typically open for the season with skiing off of the Locke Mountain Triple with midstation access and add top-to-bottom ASAP. 

Check out the Special Deals page for all our current offers and ways to save, and get ready. Opening day will be here before you know it.

Think snow!

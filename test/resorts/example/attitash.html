Comments


Spring-like temps and conditions, with 88% of our mountain open, is going to make this a soft-snow Sunday Funday. Attitash Mountain Resort is scheduled to open at 8:30 a.m. with 59 trails and 225 acres of open, skiable terrain accessed by 8 lifts; including the Kachina Triple servicing groomed terrain for novice skiers and riders at Bear Peak. 82% of our open terrain has been groomed overnight. Ungroomed terrain open is recommended for experts only and may contain some hazards in addition to moguls, please be cautious. When colder temps return our snowmaking efforts will start up again on Wandering Skis at Bear Peak as well as Whitehorse & Wilfred's on the Attitash side. A start on the Abenaki Park with medium progressive features is now open on Lower Kachina at Bear Peak.

Tickets & Specials


Full day lift tickets will be $70 Adult; $55 Young Adult; and $50 Junior/Senior. Join us this afternoon for Sunday Funday and purchase your afternoon lift ticket for just $30. Discount that even further with your Vertical Value Card. If you don't have that, be sure to check out our Vertical Value Card offer and save 10-50% on lift tickets all season long.

Parks Report


The park crew has built out the Lower Kachina portion of our new Abenaki Park with medium progressive features. Check out attitash.com for the latest feature details on the new terrain park page and to see updates on the Abenaki Terrain Park.

Events


We're in to our January 12-18 Learn2 Ski or Snowboard Free week! See website for details and sign up yourself, a family member, or friend for a FREE Learn2 Day One Clinic package including 3-hour introductory lesson, lift ticket, and rentals. Spots limited and filled on first-come, first-serve pre-registration basis.

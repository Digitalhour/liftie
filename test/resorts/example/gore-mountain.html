Gore Mountain Greetings!
We will be opening for this Saturday & Sunday!  Check out Jamboree serviced by the Sunway Chair, as well as a few park features on The Arena!  Ages 13+ can join us for just $18, and ages 7-12 just $9 with the donation of two non-perishable food items per person for our holiday drive.  We'll continue making snow around the clock and hope to open more terrain for the following weekend.
I hope you are finding your way around the site pretty well?  Let us know if you have any questions!
We added a couple of bonus days to our sales deadlines for both season passes and Mountain Adventure price deadline- you need to purchase by end-of-day Sunday for the best available price.  
Thanks for checking on Gore, we'll be back in touch with more news ASAP.

What's Happening

We will be opening for this Saturday & Sunday! Check out Jamboree serviced by the Sunway Chair, as well as a few park features on The Arena! Ages 13+ can join us for just $18, and ages 7-12 just $9 with the donation of two non-perishable food items per person for our holiday drive.

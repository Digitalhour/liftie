What's Happening

Our scenic gondola ride and summer activities season has ended- thank you for joining us for a wonderful Harvest Fest!  We are preparing for the opening of our winter season- look for snowmaking starting up during November, and Opening Day quickly following the guns turning on!  In the meantime, mark your calendar for our season pass and multi-week Snow Sports program deadline on November 17. 

Join our team and ski or ride free this winter!  The Gore Mountain Job Fair is this Saturday, 10am-1pm- learn about available positions, meet department supervisors, and submit your application!

Special Notes

We are excited for extensive renovations to three of our lodges!  The size of the Saddle Lodge has doubled and we are adding more dining options there, the original 1967 gondola unloading station at Gore's summit has been transformed into the new Straight Brook Lodge, and we're expanding the rental shop, children's program center, and restrooms of the Northwoods Lodge.  Planning your winter vacation?  Check out e-ticket savings, our long list of lodging partners, and fantastic value with our ski and stay packages!

Snowmaking starts soon-be ready for winter!  Our SKI3 Snow Samplers are just $259- grab your 4-pack of unrestricted, transferable tickets by 12/8/17!

Podcast

Late April Pod
April 16, 2017
Podcast Transcript

Thank you for tuning in, this is Emily with your More Gore podcast for the second half of the beautiful month of April. 

We can't thank our guests enough for what was a really solid winter season- while Closing Day has to come at some point every

year, this time around I am have been very sad to see it end.  We miss all of you already! 

However, the warmer months of 2017 will bring a very busy season of improvements as we prepare for next winter, several

special events for you to enjoy with us, and time to get excited about all of the awesome turns we're going to make in just a

handful of months from now, when the snow begins flying once again.

Next season's passes are on sale, and our best prices are available until August 11.  Every pass includes one built-in non-holiday

bonus ticket to share with a friend or family member, unlimited scenic rides and hiking during our summer and fall season, and

several other perks.

Be sure to check out plenty of rafting fun on the Hudson River this spring season- the 60th Annual Whitewater Derby is coming

up on May 6-7.

We happily host weddings, birthdays, reuinions, corporate parties, and other types of special events all year with catering and

recreational experiences customized to what works best for your group.  Contact our Sales Office to plan your next special

event.

Thanks again for tuning in, and make it a great day out there!

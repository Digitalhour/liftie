3S Gaislachkogl II

A11 00:00-00:00

00:00-00:00

5.5 min

Since December 2010, two modern cable cars have opened up Sölden's outstanding ski mountain - the Gaislachkogl (1,363 - 3,040 m). From the middle station a 3-S-Bahn leads to the summit of Gaislachkogl. The 3-lane section on the 2nd section has remarkable dimensions: the longest section between two supports is 1200 meters, but support 3 - the last support in front of the mountain station - measures 50 meters in height and 50 meters in length. 3-S stands for 3 ropes, 2 suspension ropes on which the 11 nacelle drive and a pull rope that moves the cabins

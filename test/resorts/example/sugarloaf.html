Today at the Loaf

Welcome Home, Sugarloafers! 

Homecoming Weekend is officially underway, and we're celebrating by taking the snow guns out for a test run. While it's still too warm for any real snow, we'll be ready to rock and roll when the season starts. Until then, we'll enjoy fall while we can with a jam-packed Homecoming schedule. 

The countdown to winter is on, and you'll want to be ready. Start planning your ski season now:

New England Pass

You know the drill: Grab your season pass before October 8th for the best price. Be sure to check out our latest addition to the lineup- the Bronze Plus Pass. It's everything you love about the Bronze pass (price) with the addition of Sundays, so your weekend plans can still involve a ski day. And if you're looking for the Millennial Pass, it's now called the 20s Pass. Same great deal, more accurate name. (Fun fact, the oldest Millennials were born in the early 80's and have grown-up jobs now). 

Lessons

Register your kids for winter junior programs by October 8th and save. Bubblecuffers, Minicuffers, Junior Professionals, All Mountain, and Big Mountain, and CVA Weekend Programs are all open for registration. Prices go up next week, so don't wait. 

Events

We've been in party-planning mode all summer, and have the event calendar to prove it. Check out all the concerts, festivals, and fun happening at the Loaf, and plan your winter accordingly. 

While we wait for winter, there's still a lot of fun to be had in the mountains: hiking, mountain biking, and golf are still in full swing, and the foliage just about at peak. Get out there and enjoy!

-Your friends at Sugarloaf

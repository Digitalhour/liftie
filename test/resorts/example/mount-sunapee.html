Mount Sunapee Snow Report

SNOWMAKING UNDERWAY ALL WEEKEND

20-30°
20"

Mount Sunapee is off to one of its best starts ever. We have top-to-bottom skiing and riding on the Main Mountain, Sunbowl and South Peak. 17 trails are open totaling over 70 acres of terrain for all abilities. This weekend 6 lifts will be operating including all three quads, the North Peak Triple chair and two carpets. We have a hiking terrain park open on lower Pipeline with 10 features. Skiing surfaces are machine groomed loose granular and frozen granular on a 10-20” base.

Snowmaking will be underway all weekend. Next week looks like some of the coldest temperatures all season. Look for a lot more terrain to open by Saturday, Dec 14.

Ticket prices are $48 for adults, $38 for young adults and seniors and $33 for juniors and super seniors.

Through December 20 we are offering a $39 Beginner Special. First-time skiers and riders ages 4 and older receive a two-hour beginner lesson, rental equipment and a ticket for South Peak for only $39. This package must be reserved and purchased in advance on-line or over the phone at 603-763-3564.

Saturday and Sunday, December 14 and 15 Mount Sunapee will be hosting Demo Days with Bob Skinner’s Ski & Sport. Demo the newest skis and snowboards from seven ski manufacturers and four snowboard manufacturers.

In an effort to help stock the local food shelters for the holidays, Sunday, December 15 is the first of two Cares & Shares Days at Mount Sunapee. Each skier or rider who brings five non-perishable food items to the ticket window can ski or ride all day for only $35.

Make Mount Sunapee your mountain getaway. The big mountain with the best snow is right in your backyard.

Snow Report: November 13 UPDATE

Snowmaking is continuing in anticipation of opening day on Thursday, November 14 at 9am. On Thursday we will be skiing on 6 slopes from midstation down with 2 lifts and 1 conveyor lift operating. Lift tickets will be $35 for adults and $25 for juniors age 5-12 and we will continue this reduced rate through Sunday. Regular rental rates will apply. Remember, we are in our early season operating hours of 9am until 4:30pm, Monday through Friday and 8:30am - 4:30pm, Saturday and Sunday. There will be no half day rates available at this time, but we will be offering our ONE RUN, WE REFUND offer as we move into the season.

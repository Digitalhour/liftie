Alta Ski Area has groomed runs off of every lift. The groomed runs on a given day will depend on weather and snow conditions. Daily groomed run reports can be found in all of Alta's restaurants, ticket offices and retail facilities. We also have 5km of groomed Nordic Track.
 
Grooming a run does not ensure the quality of the snow on the run. Please ski under control and within your ability. With snow and wind during the night, grooming may not be visible, but the surface under the new snow should be smooth. Please observe and obey all signs, rope lines, and other warnings.

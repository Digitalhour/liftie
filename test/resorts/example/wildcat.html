Comments

The mountain was alive yesterday, skiers and riders were able to enjoy every bit of it with warm temperatures and spring-like conditions. We hope you even had the chance to see the beautiful undercast during the day. The groomed runs were skiing great with soft surfaces and the ungroomed just as good with barely a dent from the precip Friday night. We're looking at another mid-40s day at the mountain so get ready for spring conditions in January. Even though we have some rain in the forecast it should hold off until the lifts have stopped spinning and you've thoroughly enjoyed your day! Wildcat Mountain will open at 8:30 a.m. (weather/ conditions permitting) with skiing/ riding on 48 trails and 223 acres of terrain accessed by 4 lifts. Primary conditions should be spring-like and variable with 22 of our open trails groomed, including top-to-bottom on Polecat as well as Middle and Lower Lynx. We have 137 acres of groomed runs making 60% of our open terrain waiting as morning cords. Middle Catapult has also seen grooming giving you more manicured routes down from the summit. Almost 50% of our open terrain is groomed with some great summit-to-base runs. Ungroomed terrain has variable conditions and is for experts only and may contain hazards, please ski/ride with caution. We'll see you on the slopes!

Parks Report

The terrain park on Lower Catapult saw a complete park rebuild. We've got a nice line starting with three jumps leading into four rail and box features. Shred Wildcat Terrain Parks today! We’ve got a dedicated park page on our website. Be sure to give it a look and check out the features.

620 Ragged Mountain Road | Danbury, NH 03230 | 603-768-3600

Located just 90 minutes from Boston in Danbury NH, Ragged Mountain Resort is the closest place in New England to lose yourself. With New Hampshire's only high-speed six pack chairlift, you can ski and ride on two mountain peaks. 50 trails include 11 glades, 3 terrain parks, and miles of groomed terrain. In the spring, summer and fall, play golf on our 18-hole, par-72 challenging course. Year-round make Ragged Mountain your setting for weddings, special occasions or corporate functions. Come on up and Get Ragged!

Special Messages

January skiing and riding at Sun Valley is the Best! The skiing and riding is fantastic with stellar conditions, on and off piste.

Remember to 'Like' the Sun Valley SnowSports School on FACEBOOK and check out the latest news, mountain pics and video.

DEMO THE LATEST EQUIPMENT TODAY! Pete Lanes, at the River Run Base area has the latest and greatest demo skis for your mountain enjoyment. Try the new skis from Head and Volkl ... you'll see the difference.

Healthy Fun ...Remember to hydrate on a regular basis.

TRAILS MERGE! ... For a safe and comfortable day, remember to 'watch out for merging traffic' before you go ... ie LOOK BEFORE YOU LEAP for your safety and the safety of those around you.

Slow Skiing / Riding Zones are there for our safety. PLEASE ski/ride SLOW in SLOW ZONES. If you don't know where slow zones are, find them on your trail map.

Roundhouse Restaurant will be closed for lunch on Mondays & Tuesdays for the month of January excluding Martin Luther King Day on Monday, 1/21.

This Week in Sun Valley

Race Information

Public NASTAR race on Sunday from 11:30am to 1pm. Sunday there is a SVSEF D-Team NASTAR race at 10:30am. NASTAR races are on Lower Warm Springs. All races are subject to weather and snow conditions. Please call the race desk for updated info at (208) 622-6356.

Lift Operating Hours

Lifts to the top of the mountain run from 9am to 3:45pm. All lower lifts run from 9am to 4pm. Access to Seattle Ridge and the bowls closes at 2:45. The last Gondola ride is at 3:45.

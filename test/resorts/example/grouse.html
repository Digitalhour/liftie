Announcements

Great news - we're excited to announce that we'll be kicking off the 2017.2018 winter season this Friday at 3pm! Skiing and riding will be available on Paradise via the Greenway Chairlift as well as on The Cut to mid-station via the Screaming Eagle Express Chairlift. The Paradise Rookie Park and The Cut Park will be open with features for all abilities. Skiing and riding will be available weekdays 3pm-10pm and weekends & holidays from 9am-10pm. Check back frequently for updates on additional terrain and extended hours. Our 8,000-square foot mountaintop Ice Skating Pond is now open 9am - 10pm! Rentals will be available from Alpine Guest Services.

Last Update: Jan 28 2013 8:30 AM

Snow News:


We have received another 3 inches overnight, and 7 inches in the past 24 hours Thank YOU mother nature! Today and the rest of the week’s forecast is calling for more snow, let’s hope this storm delivers! The weather is shaping up just right for all kinds of snowy fun. Whether you ride, ski, free heel, huck or just plain rip, it’s going to be a fun day for anything on the snow! Did you KNOW Moonlight offers guided snowshoe hikes? Tours go out every Tuesday morning at 10am from the Madison Village Base Area. It’s a great way for you and friends or family to explore Montana’s wintery landscape! Don’t forget that Super Bowl Sunday is just around the corner... Join us next Sunday, February 3 at the Headwaters Grille or at the Jack Creek Bar for food and drink specials. Enjoy cheering on your favorite team to victory! Kickoff starts at 4:30pm. Drink Bayern Faceplant and earn a chance to win a 32” Vizio TV. For every pint of Faceplant purchased at the Headwaters Grille from now until halftime of the Superbowl, you'll receive a raffle ticket to win a 32" flat screen TV. Drawing will be held at halftime during the Superbowl at Headwaters Grille. All are eligible, must be 21 or older and you must be present at the Headwaters Grille to win! The Madison Village Base Area is open and all base area services and ski-in/ski-out access are available. Tickets have reverted back to non peak pricing The Freestyle Forest, Zero Gravity, Bad Dog, and Pony terrain parks are scheduled to open with over 31 features! More features are being worked on each day, so swing by to see what the crew has been up to. Tune in here and to our Facebook and Twitter for real time condition updates and all your Moonlight preseason news. Until then...THINK SNOW!


Surface Conditions:


Powder, packed powder, and machine groomed. Please be aware that early season conditions do exist. Ski and ride with caution.

Snow Report

10:09PM Monday, October 16, 2017

The ski area is currently closed to the public for all uses. Beginning Monday, October 30th until Opening Day, NO TRAVEL on TSG property or within USFS permitted boundary is allowed due to snowmaking equipment, snowmobiles, snowcats, Howitzer use/training, and use of explosives for avalanche mitigation work.

The gondola re-opens for the winter on Friday November 17, 2017.

Mountain Report | Windham Mountain Resort
Skiing and riding through the bombogenesis of 2018 is one of those things you'll tell the grandkids about someday. Don't miss your chance, because this thing is pulling out of here tomorrow! Grayson was kind enough to drop off 6" of  fresh snow, elevating conditions to an epic level on 250 acres of open terrain across 41 trails.  9 lifts are planned to spin from 8am to 4pm Today. G lift and the East Peak are planned to open at 9am. The wind is creating some nice snow drifts out there, so if you missed out on yesterday's powder, look for some new stashes on Why Not, Widgeon, World Cup, Wildcat, Upper Warpath, Wedel and The Wall. Groomers left their tracks on everything else early this morning. Saturday's forecast calls for mostly sunny skies and a high of 5°. Note that high winds are expected today. You'll want to have some extra layers and a face mask on while you're out there cranking turns!  When it's time to warm up inside, enjoy  live music in SEASONS  and a matinee showing of "A Night At The Museum" at the Winwood Inn Movie Theater, both starting at 2pm.

Whirlwind Express (A lift) will be closed today for repairs. Mechanics are working around the clock to get the lift up and running as soon as possible, an they're just about done!

Night skiing and riding is cancelled for tonight. Lifts close at 4pm. The Adventure Park will close early at 4pm.

Tickets
We recognize that not having one of our high speed lifts open is an inconvenience, therefore, lift ticket prices have been reduced. Prices through Sunday 1/7 are: $66 for Adults, $51 for Youth, $54 for Seniors (65+), $47 for Seniors (75+), and $54 for College Students. Save 10% by purchasing your lift ticket in advance on our webstore, here. (Must purchase by 11:50pm the day before ticket date).

NASTAR & Terrain Parks
Park Crew has 25 features open across 3 terrain parks! The Mini Park on Lower Wraparound is now open with a full build. 9 Small features can be found there, while 8 small & medium features are up in Warpath Park. Wilbur Park is also up and running with 8 medium & large features. NASTAR  is now open for the season on Lower Wheelchair, from 11am - 3pm, Friday - Sunday and on holidays.

Deals

30% off stays at the Winwood Inn February 19th through February 22nd! Use promo code 30PR online or mention this promotion when calling at 518-734-3000.

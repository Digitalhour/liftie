lift safety
 It’s your responsibility to know how to use the lift safely. Need assistance? Ask the lift attendant for help. Smallest kids should load closest to the attendant. Remove and carry backpacks on your lap. Do not use phones, music, or games while loading or unloading. Please, no smoking in the lift lines or while riding the lift.

 Weather or not
 R2 is one of our patrollers. He is so passionate about weather that in his spare time, prior to an storm, he studies several forecast models to create a personalized forecast for Monarch Ski Patrol. Now he brings that forecasts to you, the public. Delve into R2's unpredictable, feverish mind...if you dare.

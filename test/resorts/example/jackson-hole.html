83 Trails Open

Jackson Hole Mountain Resort has 116 (named) trails including 50% expert, 40% intermediate and 10% beginner. The mountain features 12 lifts including Bridger Gondola and “Big Red”, our world-famous Aerial Tram. With 2500 acres of in-bound terrain and a whopping 4,139-foot vertical drop, there’s plenty of room to explore. Enjoy!

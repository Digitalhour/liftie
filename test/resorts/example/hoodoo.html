Daily Conditions Report for Sunday, December 9, 2018

Light snow showers continue here at Hoodoo increasing to a forecasted 3-6 inches this afternoon and evening. It may not be coming in fast but it does continue to trickle in as we close out opening weekend. We have sub-freezing temperatures and snow showers on the forecast for the rest of this week so the base we have should hold out and maybe even grow a bit. Let’s keep those prayers to the snow gods coming and see if we can’t get that next round of snow to yield us some feet instead of inches so we can open up more of the mountain.

Remember, we only have about 17" of snow and grooming is limited so please ride with care, watch out for unmarked obstacles, and use caution when going off piste. I can’t wait to see you at the mountain!

Today's Report

1/13/13 - 6:45AM

It is time to break out those dancin shoes and crank up the Snow Dance. The snow on the hill is holding up really well, deep and groomed to perfection but the snow in the woods is getting thin and the snow on the sides of the roads is getting yucky and depressing!  So shake it up a little...maybe at halftime tonight. Today you can expect some fog through mid morning and then cloudy skies with temps climbing into the 40's.  So get ready for some hero snow to carve up...a little moist but really sweet under foot. Come play and then head home to watch the Pats and Texans.

The NORDIC Center is OPEN...we are adding snow to the open terrain to try to keep it fresh through this January thaw.  Our Nordic trials race series is underway every Wednesday and Saturday so come on up and give it a go on Skate, Classic, or Shoe!

Please Take Note: A "Groomed Terrain" Icon next to a trail name indicates that a trail has been subject to grooming activity within the past 24 hours. Trail conditions, including groomed surfaces, change constantly due to many factors including, but not limited to, skier traffic, weather, snowmaking, and other factors, manmade and natural. This report is subject to change without notice at any time.

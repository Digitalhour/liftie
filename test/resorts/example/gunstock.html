Today's Report

3/28/16 - 12:00AM

That's all folks! Gunstock would like to thank everyone who visited us this season and stayed positive throughout one of the most difficult winters we have ever been through. The skiing may be over but more fun is just around the corner. Our summer attractions open at the end of May and the new Mountain Coaster will be opening in July. You can follow the build progress here.

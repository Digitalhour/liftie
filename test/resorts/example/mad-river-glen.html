Updated on Monday, September 23, 2013 at 13:00:00

Happy Thanksgiving Eve
 
We picked up another 5" or so overnight after the rain changed to snow yesterday afternoon. This on top of maybe 2-4" the night before and the 6-8" from the past weekend, it's starting to look legitimately winter-like here for ThanksgivinGl . While we have projected opening for the December 14-15 weekend we will crank things up sooner if Mother Nature allows. So be sure to keep an eye on the latest from The Single Chair Weather Blog and to this snow report.

Weather Details Last Update at 11:44am on 12/17/16

11:45 am UPDATE: Since lifts have been spinning, we've received 4 more inches, adding to the 2" that fell overnight!

Welcome to the weekend skiers and riders. Get ready for a snowy day on the mountain. We received 2 inches of snow last night and it is still coming down! We invite you to explore six new trails that are opening today, including World Cup, Lower Slalom Glade, and Beeline. Additionally, Tyrolienne Park is reopening with a fresh build consisting of 10 features for you to hit. (Note: an SES pass is required to enter any terrain park, so get yours here.) We have 9 lifts scheduled to run, with first chair firing up at 8:30 am and servicing 244 acres of machine groomed and packed powder terrain.

Today's forecast is calling for snow in the morning followed by light snow and flurries. Temps are expected to reach a high of 33 degrees at the summit as well as the base, with SW 20-35 mph winds at the summit and SE 10-16 mph winds at the base.

If you're looking for something to do other than ski or snowboard, head over to the Nordic Center, which is open with 3 km of Nordic skiing, 12 km of snowshoe trails and fat bikes available for rent.

The Spirits of the Season sampler kicks off at 4 pm! Get your package of 10 tasting tickets early at Stratton.com

Safety Message - Please stay on open terrain. If you duck a rope, you will lose your ticket or pass.

This report reflects the conditions as of 6 am.

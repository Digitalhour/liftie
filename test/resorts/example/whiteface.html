First Tracks Saturday/ Sunday/ Monday. For intermediate or above skill level, limited to 14 people per day. Must have valid lift ticket. Be at Guest services desk at 7:45 AM suited, booted & ready to go. Go to Guest Service desk or email ski@whiteface.com for details & to register.

Saturday: Live music in the Cloudspin Lounge with Organized Chaos at 3pm plus Kahlua specials and giveaways

Fed 3: Super Football Sunday. With discounted lift tickets of $40 adult, $35 Teens/Seniors & $30 Juniors. The Live Owl Band performs live in the Cloudspin Lounge at 3:00 PM. Also,Bud and Bud Light specials and giveaways.

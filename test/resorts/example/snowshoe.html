Weather Details Last Update at 8:03am on 12/17/16

Our terrain expansion continues today with two fun new Runs-- Lower Ballhooter and Singletree. It's going to be a wet one out there today, but warmer than the past few days, so grab your rain gear and get out on the mountain to carve up some new terrain!

Robertson's Run Park in Silver Creek opened for the season yesterday, and is open until 9 pm, when there is less chance of rain! Progression Park is open with 14 features, including 4 jumps and 10 jibs. Early Season Rail Jam goes down at 3 p.m. today in front of Shaver's Center. Be sure to go by, check it out, cheer on the riders, and then head over to Winter Brew Fest to sample some of West Virginia's best brews!

Our 3forAll pass is a great way to save on your next trip to the mountain. With it you get 3 days of Skiing or Riding for just $159!

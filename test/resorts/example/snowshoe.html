    Snow Conditions

    Powder day! We've got a fresh blanket of the fine fluffy stuff this morning (6 inches), with additional snowfall forecasted up until around noon. Head out there this morning and see if you can find some freshies. Low temperatures and great conditions are waiting your arrival. And with the opening of Flying Eagle at Silver Creek yesterday there is 242 glorious acres to explore. Flying Eagle, Camp 99, Grabhammer, and Knot Bumper all have been set aside for taking your shots at some bump runs.

    Widowmaker will open at noon again today due to race practice. All the parks at Snowshoe are open, ready and waiting with plenty of jumps, hips and rails. Groomer's Choice is Camp 99.

    Open Terrain:
    UPDATES:

    Mountaineer Parks has opened our brand new Skill Builder Park at Silver Creek. It is built on a concept that we call “Terrain Based Learning." The park has very gentle rollers, mellow banked turns and small basic shapes. These elements help teach the core elements of skiing and riding. There are no rails, boxes or jumps. This is a great place for all skiers and riders to hone their skills and build to the next level.

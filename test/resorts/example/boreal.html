Daily Operations Report

News & Conditions

WE'RE OPENING THIS FRIDAY!

We're excited to announce that we're opening for the 2014-15 winter season this Friday, November 7 at 9:00am. We'll offer one run with six terrain park features accessed by the Castle Peak Quad chairlift. Early season conditions will exist. No easier terrain will be available at this time.

EARLY-SEASON RATES & HOURS:

Adult (18+): $39 all-day; $29 night-only.
Teen (13-17): $39 all-day; $29 night-only.
Child (5-12): $29 all-day.
Young Child (4 & under): $5 all-day.

All-Day tickets are valid from 9:00am - 9:00pm. Night-Only tickets are valid from 3:30pm- 9:00pm.

AVAILABLE SERVICES:

  • The Boreal Base Lodge will be open for tickets, rentals, guest services, retail and restrooms.
  • Snowsports School will offer private lessons only. Standard rates apply and based on availability. On-site purchase only.
  • Guests may pick-up season passes at Mountain Services, open from 8:00am – 9:00pm.
  • Lift tickets will be sold at the outside Ticket Windows from 8:00am – 9:00pm.
  • The Woodward Tahoe Bunker will continue daily operations with hours from 11:00am – 8:00pm.
  • The Boreal Restaurant and Boreal Bar & Grill will be open for food and drinks.
  • Moondoe’s Cafe, located in the Woodward Tahoe Bunker, will be open serving specialty coffees, sandwiches and other snacks.
